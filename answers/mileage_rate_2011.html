Beginning Jan. 1, to Jun 30 2011, the standard mileage rates are: 51 cents per mile for business miles driven. 19 cents per mile driven for medical or moving purposes. 14 cents per mile driven in service of charitable organizations. Beginning July. 1, to Dec 31 2011, the standard mileage rates are: 55.5 cents per mile for business miles driven. 23.5 cents per mile driven for medical or moving purposes. 14 cents per mile driven in service of charitable organizations.
