The Natural Language Classifier service helps to reduce the workload of a live support staff by answering questions quickly and accurately and by taking predictive actions, such as routing questions to live agents in real time.

Also, you can use the service to:

  • Classify tweets into categories like news, events, or opinions
  • Classify text messages into categories like personal, work, or promotions
  • Classify text from social media outlets and other sources to identify whether the text relates positively or negatively to an offering or a service
